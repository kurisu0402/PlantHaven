Questions

Find answers to common questions about plant-based sustainability and environmental conservation.

How can plants help reduce air pollution?

Plants absorb pollutants through their leaves, helping to purify the air and improve air quality.

What are the benefits of using organic fertilizers?

Organic fertilizers promote soil health and reduce the risk of chemical runoff, supporting a balanced ecosystem.

How can I start a sustainable garden at home?

Begin by choosing native plants, composting, and using water-efficient gardening techniques.
